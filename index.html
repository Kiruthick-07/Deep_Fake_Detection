What Is DeepFake ?


Face swap , deepfakes are designed to replace a person’s face in a photo with another, creating content that appears authentic but is entirely fabricated. Face swap deepfakes have been used to spread misinformation, manipulate public opinion, damage reputations , and create malicious or harmful. Reports indicate that the number of deepfake videos online has been doubling annually, with over 85,000 deepfake videos detected in 2023 , compared to just 7,964 in 2019. Without effective ways to detect them, the potential for misuse continues to grow , posing a significant challenge for individuals and organizations alike.
